PUSH TO MASTER

My Journey From Healthcare to Software Engineering

In 2018, I was working as a medication historian in a Philadelphia emergency room while suffering from severe leg issues that resulted in chronic pain that made it near impossible to do my job. From 2019-2020, I was unemployed and recovering from surgery while teaching myself as much programming as I possibly could. Finally, in 2021, I decided to join a coding bootcamp called Tech Elevator to accelerate my growth and get a job in software engineering as soon as possible. This blog is a way for me to ruminate about the journey and chronicle the process for other bootcamp prospects and self-taught developers.
