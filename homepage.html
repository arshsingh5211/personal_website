I am a 32-year old former medication historian and current software engineer from Detroit, Michigan. While I enjoyed working in an Emergency Department and helping patients in their times of need, I eventually realized that my true passion lies in the world of computer programming. I began my career transition by self-learning using the countless online resources available, and then accelerated my skills by taking college courses and attending Tech Elevator, one of the best coding bootcamps in the midwestern United States. I created this website to display my portfolio, share my experiences, and hopefully provide a valuable resource for other potential programmers looking for some guidance.
